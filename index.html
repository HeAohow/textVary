textVary

Help you write better GRE/TOEFL essays by detecting your repeating phrases.

What is textVary

In GRE/TOEFL writing, a varied sentence structure and vocabulary often helps you a lot to receive a high score. And here comes textVary, a writing assistant program that is capable of detecting your repeating phrases and providing you with visual feedback, thus helping you form a habit of using diverse expressions.

The following video demonstrates how textVary helps the user avoid a second of of the phrase 'as well as'.

TextVary can also recognize the various forms of the same word.

In fact, textVary analyzes your essay carefully, it even understands the same punctuation may have different meaning in different contexts.
